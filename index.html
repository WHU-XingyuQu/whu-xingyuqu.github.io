About Me

I am a second-year undergraduate student in the Hongyi Honor Class, School of Computer Science at Wuhan University. I joined the Luojia Totem Database Lab in my freshman year, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sheng Wang.

My research focuses on the systematic optimization of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) in high-concurrency scenarios, aiming to build efficient and "intelligent" data management systems. I'm also passionate about Database Systems and LLM infrastructure (e.g., KV-cache optimization).
