About

Benxi Senior School 2021–2024

I focus on system optimization of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) in high-concurrency scenarios, multi-query scheduling of vector databases, throughput improvement, and other related issues. The goal is to achieve synergistic optimization of retrieval and inference, and to develop system work that is deployable, reproducible, and scalable.

Research Interests

  • Multi-Query Concurrency and Throughput Optimization for RAG systems (multi-vector query, batching, scheduling).
  • Robustness and engineering implementation of vector retrieval structures (e.g., HDR-Tree, FAISS Indexes) on high-dimensional data.
  • Joint Optimization between inference (LLM Decoding) and retrieval (kNN/Subgraph/Caching).
  • Cache Consistency and Reuse Strategy under KV-cache / Retrieval Cache / Semantic Sharding.
